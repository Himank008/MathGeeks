Profit and Loss
*Some Important things to know*
  • The amount paid to purchase an or the price at which an article is made, is known as its cost price and is abbreviated as C.P.
  • The price at which an article is sold is called its selling price and is abbreviated as S.P.
  • If the selling price is greater than the cost price, the difference between the cost price and selling price is called Profit.
  • If the cost price is greater than the selling price, the difference between the cost price and selling price is called Loss.
  • In malls and departmental stores, every item is tagged with a card and its price is written on it which is called Marked Price(M.P.)
  • In order to increase the sale or clear the old stock, sometimes the shopkeepers offer a certain percentage of rebate on the marked price.This rebate is called discount.
